What TurtleSync Does

TurtleSync is a student led project designed to support sea turtle protection by making it easier to report injured turtles, disturbed nests, or hatchlings in danger. The project focuses on turning concern into clear information that conservation teams can use.

  • Collects basic details about injured or nesting sea turtles.
  • Organizes location and conditions in a clear and consistent format.
  • Helps rescue and conservation teams respond more quickly and effectively.
  • Raises awareness about safe and legal ways to help protected species.

How Reporting Works

Reporting through TurtleSync is designed to be quick, clear, and safe for both the person and the turtle.

  1. Observe safely. Keep your distance and do not touch, move, or crowd the animal.
  2. Submit a report. Use the TurtleSync form to share what you see, where you are, and any important details about the turtle condition or behavior.
  3. Add photos if possible. Photos help responders understand the situation without disturbing the animal.
